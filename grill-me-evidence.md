Grill-me Evidence

Original idea
A country insights app using REST Countries API with search, comparison, AI summaries, and Q&A.

What grill-me challenged
- The scope was too broad with too many features.
- Open-ended Q&A was risky and not needed for a proof-of-concept.
- Comparison feature could become too complex.
- The ETL and AI layer needed clearer definition.

What I changed
- Reduced the app to 3–4 core features only.
- Removed open-ended Q&A.
- Simplified comparison to basic fields only.
- Defined that AI summaries must be grounded in processed data only.

Final scope
- Search a country
- View country profile
- Compare 2 countries
- Generate short AI summary
